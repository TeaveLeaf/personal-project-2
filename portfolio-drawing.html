This is a logo I designed for a family member’s clothing brand. The logo was meant to represent her clothing brand and make her store’s page look more professional. I chose to show this art piece because I wanted to show my ability to come up with and design logos for brands and other organizations. I think I did a good job on it, and she still continues to use the logo I made for her as the representative of her clothing brand today.
